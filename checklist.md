- sign online forms
- watch briefing video

- footstool
- roof kit w/tools
- long cable lock w/key

- screwdriver

- 4L water
- spare towel

- throw rope

- bike
- spare clothing
- spare eyewear

### Transition Bag

- magnetic key box
- 2 filled water bottles
- whistle
- bike helmet
- bike shoes
- running shoes
- socks

- floor mat

### Startline Bag

- multisport gloves
- neoprene boots

### Schedule

- T-90min: find parking
- assemble cradles and cable locks
- equip bike with water bottle and whistle
- T-60min: wear face mask
- pickup race kit
- wear bib
- position cradles and cable locks
- apply stickers
- drop-off bike and transition bag
- equip boat with carabiner
- wear timing chip
- wear gloves, boots, PFD, bib
- secure car and key
- launch
